Projects

Interactive Fractal Viewer

A website made with p5.js to generate an interactive fractal tree.

Conway's Game of Life

An implementation of the classic cellular automata, Conway's Game of Life, along with a glitch that is cool enough to get it's own gif. Written in Processing using an object-oriented implementation.

Perlin Noise Art

Randomly generated art, using the classic Perlin Noise algorithm in p5.js! It's relatively simple, but I think it's too cool not to show off.

Glitch Art

Some of my favorite pieces of glitch art, taken by & of me and glitched using a channel shifting algorithm in Processing.

Tide Pod Synthesizer

A synthesizer built into a tide pod case, powered by an arduino (link to code). Not many materials available (made at the beginning of the pandemic), but I am very proud of the functionality that I managed to get out of it.
