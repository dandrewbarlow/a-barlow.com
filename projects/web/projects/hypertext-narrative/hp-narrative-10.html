The Future of the Internet: 10

Like the fateful Tower of Babel, the internet’s success also drives people apart. Anonymity allows for a wealth of unverifiable information, and brings out the worst side in people. I think in the future people will catch on, but the internet as a disinformation tool is the unfortunate reality we find ourselves in today. Until political meme pages are viewed the same way as deposed Nigerian princes, this will probably continue. I’m hopeful that day will come soon however.

Written by Andrew Barlow
